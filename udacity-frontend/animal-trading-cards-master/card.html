Rabbit

A male rabbit is called a buck; a female is called a doe..

  • Family: Leporidae
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Lagomorpha
  • Kingdom: Animalia

Rabbits, also known as bunnies or bunny rabbits, are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha Oryctolagus cuniculus includes the European rabbit species and its descendants, the world's 305 breeds[1] of domestic rabbit.
